UNSC

The United Nations Security Council is the only committee in the United Nations with the ability to impose sanctions and/or military action against member states in the case of urgent international crises. This fast-paced, intense committee will deal with The Situation in Mali. The UN Security Council is broadly tasked with maintaining world peace. As the only UN body with binding power, the Security Council is the most influential committee in the United Nations. States in this committee will be expected to not only represent their national interests but bear the responsibility of crafting practical solutions to some of the world’s most pressing problems.Delegates in this committee will have to work within their country’s policy and the committee’s mandate to solve some of the world’s fast-developing international security crises.
