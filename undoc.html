UNODC

The United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime was created to better regulate the trafficking and abuse of illicit drugs across international borders, as well as greater crime prevention, improved criminal justice, prevention of international terrorism and political corruption. The United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime strives to fight against illegal drugs and international crime. It is also the number one programme to fight terrorism in the United Nations and in the world . The UNODC was founded in 1997, its headquarters are located in Vienna, and their offices are established in New York and in Brussels. UNODC works to strengthen international action against illicit drug production, trafficking and any other drug related crime. To be able to find a solution to each of these aims, the UNODC has done a wide range of actions like keep track of illegal drugs, along with alternating in the area of unauthorized drug crops and operations against money laundering
